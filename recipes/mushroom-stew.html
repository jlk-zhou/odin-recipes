Mushroom Stew

Description

Just your ordinary mushroom stew inspired by Minecraft, a popular game. Best served with croutons and dry moo-shroom. Vegan and cruelty-free.

Ingredients

  • Ordinary mushroom
  • Milk and moo-shroom from moo-shroom
  • Croutons
  • Black pepper (because why not? )
  • Other vegetables you like

Steps

  1. Download Java and Minecraft. Create a new world.
  2. Find a moo-shroom, milk it, then butcher it to get moo-shroom.
  3. Teleport back from the virtual world to the real world. This is the key to keep this dish cruelty-free and vegan.
  4. Organize your gathered ingredients and cook them inside a pot.
  5. Blend the content into a soup, season and serve with croutons.
