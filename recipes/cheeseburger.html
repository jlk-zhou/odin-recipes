Cheeseburger


Description

Just a plain cheeseburger. Simple but comforting, can easily beat your mom's cooking. Image for reference only.

Ingredients

  • Fresh ground beef, 200g
  • Top hamburger bread, 1
  • Bottom hamburger bread, 1
  • Your favorite cheese, a guilty amount
  • Vegetables, none

Steps

  1. Shape ground beef into two patties.
  2. Add some oil into a pan, put in those patties, and sear.
  3. Press down the patties hard to create crust.
  4. While the patties are cooking, toast buns. Can add butter if you want.
  5. You can melt your cheese on top of patties in the pan or don't.
  6. Assemble the burger and eat.
